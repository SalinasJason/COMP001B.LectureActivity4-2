Honda Motor

社, Hepburn: Honda Giken Kōgyō Kabushiki gaisha, lit. 'Honda Institute of Technology and Industry Joint-Stock Company', IPA: [honda] ⓘ; /ˈhɒndə/) is a Japanese public multinational conglomerate manufacturer of automobiles, motorcycles, and battery-powered equipment, founded in October 1946 by Soichiro Honda and headquartered in Minato, Tokyo, Japan.
